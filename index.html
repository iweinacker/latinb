About us

WHO ARE WE?

We are a vibrant community of Latin American people living in Berlin. Despite the distance from our home countries, we remain deeply connected to our rich cultural heritage.

PURPOSE

Our purpose is to foster an active and inclusive community of Latin Americans in Berlin. We aim to promote our diverse cultures, traditions, and values within the city.

WHAT WILL YOU FIND HERE?

Currently, you will find a variety of Latin restaurants and cafes that offer authentic cuisine and warm hospitality. However, our vision extends beyond that. We are committed to expanding our community platform to include a wide range of shops, services, and exciting events.

HOW CAN I BE PART OF THIS COMMUNITY?

Becoming part of our community is easy! Simply visit our sign-up page and subscribe. If you have a business or event you’d like to promote, leave a comment, and we’ll be sure to get in touch with you. To access the subscription page directly, click here!!


We look forward to welcoming you to our vibrant Latin community in Berlin!
