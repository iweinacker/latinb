About us

Who are we?

We are a community of Latinamerican people living in Berlin where, despite the distance, we remain connected to our culture.

Purpose

Our purpose is to create an active community of Latin Americans where we can promote our culture here in Berlin.

What will you find here?

For now, you will only find latin restaurants and cafes, but our idea is to keep expanding so that you can also find shops of all kinds and events.

How can I be part of this community?

Very easy!! You just have to subscribe on the sign up page and, if you want to promote your business or event, write in the comment so that we can contact you. If you want to go to the subscription page you must go to the corresponding page or just click here!!
